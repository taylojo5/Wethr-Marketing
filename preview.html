Editing a Block

Inline block configuration

Clicking any block expands its configuration panel in place — no modal, no page navigation. Fields are specific to the block type.

The Worship Set block shows song list, key, tempo. The Sermon block shows series name, scripture reference, and speaker. Custom blocks are freeform.

Surface 07

Mobile & PWA

Wethr is a full PWA — installable on any iOS or Android home screen. Volunteers never need to log in through a browser. The app opens, their schedule is there.

No App Store required. Wethr installs as a PWA directly from the browser. Push notifications work on iOS 16.4+ and all modern Android devices. The volunteer experience is fully functional offline.

Offline Support

Works without internet. Because Sunday mornings don't wait.

The service worker caches the current volunteer's schedule and the next service's Order of Service. If someone opens the app in the parking lot with no signal, they see exactly what they need.

RSVP responses and messages made offline are queued and sent automatically when connectivity returns.

No login screen on repeat opens. The session persists for 30 days — volunteers tap the icon and land directly on their schedule.
